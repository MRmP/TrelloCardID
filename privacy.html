Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Card ID Power-Up ("the Power-Up") is a Trello Power-Up that assigns unique sequential IDs to cards on your Trello boards.

Data we collect

The Power-Up stores only the following data via Trello's built-in Power-Up storage:

Board-level settings: Your chosen prefix, postfix, number format, badge color, and a mapping of Trello card IDs to their assigned sequential numbers. This data is stored within Trello's own infrastructure and is only accessible to members of your board.

Data we do not collect

The Power-Up does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. It does not use cookies, analytics, tracking, or any external servers. No data ever leaves Trello's infrastructure.

Third-party services

The Power-Up is hosted on GitHub Pages solely to serve static files. GitHub may collect standard web server access logs (IP address, browser type, page requested). See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

Data retention

All data is stored within your Trello board. Disabling or removing the Power-Up from a board will make the data inaccessible. Trello's own data retention policies apply.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact the Power-Up author through the Trello Power-Up directory.
